MULTI SENSORY SPACE

Date: 18th - 19th April 2024

Time: 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Venue: E Block, EF 03, MRIIRS

About the event

Immerse yourself in a multisensory and experiential space design that delves deep into the environmental impact and aftermath of the Uttarakhand tragedy. Through captivating visuals, immersive soundscapes, and tactile elements, this space offers a profound exploration of the interconnectedness between human actions and the natural world. Visitors will embark on a sensory journey, gaining insight into the fragility of our environment and the devastating consequences of unchecked development. With a focus on education and reflection, this design aims to inspire awareness, empathy, and action towards sustainable practices and disaster prevention in vulnerable regions like Uttarakhand.
