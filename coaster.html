About the event

The DIY Coaster Making Workshop with Resin is an exciting hands-on activity designed to introduce participants to the fascinating world of resin art. Hosted as part of the college design festival, this workshop offers attendees the opportunity to unleash their creativity and craft unique coasters using resin, pigments, and various embellishments. This workshop is designed to foster creativity, experimentation, and practical skills among participants. Through this hands-on experience, participants will learn basic techniques of resin art, including mixing, pouring, and adding embellishments. Moreover, the workshop aims to encourage students to explore their creativity and express their unique design aesthetics.

Participants will gain practical experience in working with resin, a popular medium in contemporary art and design. They will develop basic skills in resin art, including mixing, pouring, and finishing. Participants will unleash their creativity and produce personalized coasters reflecting their individual design styles. Participants will have the opportunity to engage with peers and instructors, fostering a sense of community and collaboration within the School of Design. This workshop offers an exciting opportunity for students to explore their creative potential and acquire valuable skills in resin art.

Join our DIY coaster-making workshop at the college design festival! Learn to craft unique coasters from resin, unleashing your creativity while mastering resin techniques. Perfect for beginners and enthusiasts alike, this hands-on experience promises to add flair to your decor and expand your design skills. Don't miss out!
