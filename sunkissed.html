Sun-Kissed: Summer college fashion 2024

Registration Fee: Rs. 2000 (per team)

Date: 19 April 2024

Time: 1:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m.

Venue: A Block Auditorium, MRIIRS

About the event

Summer Fashion Showcase’ Calling all design students to Join ‘Sun-Kissed’ a fashion Show cum competition where you'll strut your stuff with trendy and practical college wear. Show off your skills, embrace college culture, and push the limits of collegiate fashion with your unique designs.

Step into the spotlight and compete in our Fashion Show Competition focused on Summer College Fashion. Show off your creativity and trendsetting expertise by crafting designs that capture the essence of campus life in the extreme heat of summer. From breezy ensembles to innovative accessories, contestants will compete for the title with their interpretation of the hottest seasonal trends. Embrace sustainability, inclusivity, and innovation as you showcase your unique take on collegiate style. Join us for an electrifying showdown where the boldest and most inventive designs reign supreme.

This competition provides a platform for aspiring designers to demonstrate their creativity, craftsmanship, and understanding of current fashion trends while celebrating the vibrant spirit of college life. Through "College Chic," we aim to inspire students to explore innovative design concepts, experiment with textiles, clothing and redefine the boundaries of collegiate fashion.

Competition Guidelines:.

Eligibility: The competition is open to students currently enrolled in any domains of Higher education Institutions - University or Institutions / design schools / fashion-related programs at the undergraduate or graduate level.

The theme of "College Chic" revolves around Spring/Summer college wear, encompassing casual, comfortable, and stylish attire suitable for everyday campus life. The collections May be as any / all of the following

  • Casual outfits suitable for daytime activities such as classes, studying, or hanging out with friends.

  • Dressier attire suitable for special events, presentations, or social gatherings on campus.

  • Sporty and functional outfits suitable for workouts, sports activities, or athleisure.

  • Innovative and avant-garde designs that push the boundaries of traditional college wear.

Design Criteria:

Participants will showcase minimum 4 designs in the collection Entries will be evaluated based on creativity, wearability, coherence with the theme, craftsmanship, and overall presentation.

Judging Process:

A panel of industry professionals, fashion designers, and faculty members will evaluate the entries based on the judging criteria.

  • Creativity and Originality

  • Relevance to the theme

  • Wearability for summer

  • Innovation in design

  • Presentation skill on the ramp

Fashion Show:

  • The design entries will be featured in a live fashion show event, where participants along with their models will have the opportunity to showcase their creations on the runway in front of a live audience.

Model Selection:

  • Participants can choose their models from fellow students or friends and family

  • Encourage diversity in model selection to represent a wide range of styles and body types.

Accessories and Beauty Standards:

  • Emphasize the importance of accessories that complement the summer theme.

  • Use vibrant, summer-inspired makeup and hairstyles

Music and Atmosphere:

  • Choose upbeat and summer-themed music to enhance the atmosphere..

Strict Guidelines:

  • All the teams will get 5-10 minutes to showcase their collection. So they have to prepare their ramp walk accordingly..

  • Music system will be provided by us but students will get their own playlist in pendrives according to their theme and preparation.

  • Students will be getting their own models, makeup, accessories and costumes.

Prizes:

  • 1st Prize Rs. 30000/- + certificates

  • 2nd Prize: Rs. 15000/- + certificates
