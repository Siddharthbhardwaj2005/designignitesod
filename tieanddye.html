About the event

The art of tie and dye, known for its vibrant colors and intricate patterns, holds a special place in India's rich textile heritage. This workshop offers participants the opportunity to learn from local artisans and explore the traditional techniques of tie and dye. The primary objective of the tie and dye workshop is to provide participants with practical experience in tie and dye techniques under the guidance of skilled local artisans. Through this immersive workshop, participants will not only learn the technical aspects of tie and dye but also gain insights into the cultural significance of this age-old craft.

The beauty of this art is that once you learn the process of dyeing the fabric, you will enjoy exploring endless results. It's stress relieving and meditative. It reflects your personality because no two pieces of natural dyed and shibori textiles will ever look the same. It's going to be as unique as you.

The workshop will promote cultural appreciation and awareness of traditional Indian textile crafts. Participants will gain insights into the creative process and cultural significance of tie and dye. The workshop will serve as a platform for fostering collaboration and exchange between artisans and participants.
