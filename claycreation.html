About the event

Pottery stands as one of the oldest and most cherished crafts, embodying the essence of creativity, craftsmanship, and cultural heritage. This workshop offers a unique opportunity for participants to engage directly with local artisans and explore the art of pottery-making.The objective of the pottery workshop is to provide participants with hands-on experience in pottery-making techniques under the guidance of skilled local artisans. Through this immersive experience, participants will not only learn the fundamentals of pottery but also gain insights into the cultural significance of this ancient craft.

This workshop is for all who want to try their hands on pottery. It is open to people of all skill levels. No prior experience is required to participate in this workshop. In a one-time pottery workshop, you will learn the basic techniques for working with clay and creating pottery pieces. You will learn how to prepare clay for use, how to center it on the pottery wheel.
