Bamboo Installation

Date: 18 - 19 April 2024

Time: 10:00 a.m. to 03:30 p.m.

About the event

Bamboo installation is to create a visually stunning and thought-provoking art piece that highlights the versatility, resilience, and sustainability of bamboo. Raise awareness about the environmental benefits of bamboo as a renewable and biodegradable material.Inspire creativity and innovation in utilising bamboo as a medium for artistic expression.
