About the event

The attendees of the workshop will be exposed to the ways to use CorelDraw and incorporate it in their daily lives. They will create their own designs on CorelDraw. On Day 2 they will be introduced to Laser Printing where they will get the experience of Laser Printing their own design.

Rules and Regulations:

  • Students will be given a certain size of 6 inches by 6 inches to laser cut the sheet.

  • They would have to fit the design in that particular given size.

What Participants needs to do:

  • Day 1, the attendees will learn CorelDraw and create a personal design.

  • Day 2, the attendees will learn Laser Printing and Laser Print their own design live.
