About the event

Birds play a vital role in maintaining ecological balance, and providing them with safe nesting spaces is crucial for their survival. This workshop aims to showcase the ingenuity of artisans in repurposing waste materials to create eco-friendly bird nests.We are delighted to host a unique workshop focused on building bird nests from coconut husk. This workshop offers participants the opportunity to learn from skilled artisans who specialize in repurposing natural materials to create eco-friendly habitats for birds.

The idea of the CocoNests workshop is to introduce participants to the traditional craft of bird nest building using coconut husk, a sustainable and biodegradable material. Through hands-on learning and guided instruction from local artisans, participants will gain practical skills and knowledge about creating bird-friendly habitats while promoting environmental sustainability.

Participants will develop practical skills in crafting bird nests from sustainable materials such as coconut husk. The workshop will raise awareness about the importance of providing habitat for birds and promoting biodiversity in urban environments. Collaborative efforts between artisans and participants will contribute to the creation of eco-friendly habitats that support local bird populations. The workshop will inspire participants to incorporate sustainable design practices into their own projects and advocate for environmental stewardship in their communities.
