Student Work’s Exhibition

Date: 18th - 19th April 2024

Time: 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Venue: E-Block - Ground floor entrance, First floor corridor, Second floor corridor

About the event

Design Ignite celebrates creativity and innovation in design by showcasing student’s work, which highlights the talent and potential of young designers in making.

Sheets, models, art work, digital work, competition entries all are displayed to convey the quality of education provided by the institutes. Work will be displayed covering the verticals of Interior design, Communication design, Animation and VFX design.
