About the event

The workshop aims to provide participants with comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience in utilizing green screen technology for visual effects (VFX) production. Participants will learn the fundamental principles of green screen filming, key techniques for achieving realistic VFX compositions, and practical tips for optimizing workflow efficiency.

Rules and Regulations:

a. Wear clothes without having blue and green color

b. Avoid wearing reflecting accessories like sunglasses, pen, wrist watches etc.

c. In case of long hairs, tie it properly

d. Bring accessories / dress for any particular shoot (optional).

Contents to be delivered:

  • How the setup works for green screen

  • How to key the background

  • How the layering works

  • How to composite new background

  • How to render final output
