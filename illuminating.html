Illuminating Perspectives

Date: 18th - 19th April 2024

Time: 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Venue: E Block, EF 03, MRIIRS

About the event

As part of the Design Festival organized by the School of Design at Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies (MRIIRS), we are excited to present an innovative installation titled "Illuminating Perspectives." This installation explores the intersection of geometry, light, and spatial design, offering a captivating visual experience for festival attendees.

The installation features a striking chandelier composed entirely of geometric shapes, meticulously arranged to create a dynamic interplay of form, color and light. Inspired by the principles of geometry and mathematical precision, the installation embodies a harmonious balance between simplicity and complexity. The installation features: Geometric Composition, Play of Light, Interactive Experience and Symbolism.

"Illuminating Perspectives" seeks to inspire dialogue and exploration among festival attendees, prompting them to reconsider their perception of space, form, color and light. By showcasing the integral beauty of geometric shapes and their transformative potential in design, the installation sparks curiosity and invites viewers to reimagine their surroundings in new and unexpected ways.Join us as we celebrate the power of geometry to inspire awe, provoke thought, and foster connections among diverse communities.
