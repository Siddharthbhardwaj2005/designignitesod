About the event

Gift wrapping is an art form that adds an extra layer of joy and excitement to gift-giving occasions. It serves as a visual representation of the care and thoughtfulness put into selecting and presenting a gift. Recognizing the creativity and skill involved in gift wrapping, we propose organising a Gift Wrapping Packaging Design Competition.
