Handircraft exhibition by Artisans

Date: 18 - 19 April 2024

Time: 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.

Venue: T Block, MRIIRS

About the event

The School of Design at Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies (MRIIRS) is proud to present a unique showcase of India's vibrant cultural heritage through a Handicraft Exhibition. This exhibition aims to celebrate the rich diversity and craftsmanship of local artisans from various regions across India. As part of our Design Festival, we seek to create a platform that not only honors traditional artistry but also fosters a deeper appreciation for indigenous skills and creativity.The objectives of this exhibition are:

Showcase Diversity

Highlight the diverse range of handicrafts from different states and regions of India, encompassing various techniques, materials, and cultural motifs.

Promote Cultural Exchange

Facilitate interactions between local artisans and the broader community, encouraging dialogue and understanding of the cultural significance behind each craft.

Support Artisan Communities

Provide a platform for local artisans to exhibit and sell their creations, thus promoting sustainable livelihoods and preserving traditional craftsmanship

Through this Handicraft Exhibition, we aim to exhibit an experience that not only celebrates India's rich heritage but also promotes sustainable practices and supports local artisan communities. Join us in this journey of exploration, appreciation, and innovation as we come together to celebrate the creativity and ingenuity of India's local artisans.
