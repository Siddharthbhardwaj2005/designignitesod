Reimagining Fashion

About the event

The School of Design at Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies (MRIIRS) is delighted to present an enlightening installation titled "Reimagining Fashion: A Journey Towards Sustainability" as part of our Design Festival. This installation is a testament to our commitment to promoting sustainable practices within the fashion industry and inspiring positive change.

"Reimagining Fashion" is an immersive installation that takes viewers on a transformative journey, exploring the evolution of fashion towards sustainability. Through a series of interactive exhibits, it showcases innovative approaches to design, production, and consumption that prioritize environmental responsibility, social equity, and ethical practices. The installation features: Sustainable Materials, Circular Fashion Experience and Innovation.This installation aims to ignite a movement towards a more sustainable and inclusive fashion industry, where creativity, ethics, and environmental stewardship converge
