About the event

A Master Class in Interior Design" offers a comprehensive learning experience designed to empower participants with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in the field of interior design. Through a combination of theoretical insights, practical exercises, and industry exposure, participants will emerge as proficient designers capable of creating innovative, functional, and visually compelling interiors that resonate with clients and enrich the human experience. Join us on a transformative journey as we unlock the secrets to crafting spaces that inspire, delight, and endure.
