About the event

The Conceptual Packaging Design Competition invites designers to showcase their creativity by developing innovative and functional packaging concepts. This design competition challenges participants to reimagine parking sheds with a focus on sustainability, durability, space management, and material exploration. The aim is to create a forward-thinking parking structure that not only provides shelter for vehicles but also minimises environmental impact, maximises longevity, optimises space utilisation, and explores innovative materials and construction methods.
